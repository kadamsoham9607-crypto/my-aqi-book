# Results

## Observations
- **Delhi** consistently shows the highest AQI values, indicating severe pollution.
- **Mumbai** and **Nagpur** show moderate levels, with seasonal spikes.
- **Pune** maintains relatively lower AQI, but still exceeds safe thresholds at times.

## Visual Highlights
- Line charts reveal seasonal fluctuations.
- Bar charts compare city averages.
- Heatmaps show regional hotspots.

These results highlight the urgent need for targeted interventions in high-risk areas.
