Methodology

The analysis follows a systematic workflow:

  1. Data Collection

    • Sources: Government AQI monitoring stations, open datasets, or APIs.

    • Parameters: PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, SO₂, CO, O₃.

  2. Data Cleaning

    • Handling missing values.

    • Normalizing units.

    • Removing anomalies.

  3. Data Analysis

    • Statistical summaries.

    • Time-series analysis.

    • Comparative study across cities.

  4. Visualization

    • Graphs and charts for trends.

    • Heatmaps for spatial distribution.

This methodology ensures reproducibility and clarity in results.
